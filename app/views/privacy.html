PRIVACY POLICY

PayWhere takes your privacy very seriously and is committed to protecting your privacy and providing a means to allow you to safely make purchases on multiple platforms including but not limited to Facebook, Blogger, LiveJournal, Tumblr, Wordpress, Wix, Weebly and Joomla. This privacy statement describes how we collect and use the personal information you provide to PayWhere through our platform, TackThis. It also describes the choices available to you regarding our use of your personal information and the steps you can take to access this information and to ensure that it is accurate.

  • Collection and Use of Personal Information

    We collect and retain the following personal information when you make a purchase: Contact information such as your name, email address and shipping address.

    We use the information we capture and collect to:

    • facilitate your transactions with our merchants

    • retain your delivery address information to pre-populate the checkout form for the convenience of future product purchases

    • retain items that you placed in your shopping cart, through the use of HTML5 Local Storage technology, if you leave the cart before completing your purchase

    • contact you with PayWhere's newsletters, marketing, or promotional materials and other

  • Information Sharing

    We will share your personal information with third parties (such as delivery details to the merchant to facilitate a purchase) only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

    We may also disclose your personal information
    as required by law,

    • when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request,

    • if PayWhere is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our website of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your personal information,

    • to any other third party with your prior consent to do so.

    To help protect your privacy, we allow our merchants only limited access to your contact, delivery and financial information to facilitate your transactions. The use of your information is also subject to the merchant's privacy statements. We encourage you to carefully read the privacy statement of any merchant you choose to make purchases from.

  • Payment Gateways

    Our merchants use payment gateways like PayPal via TackThis to collect and process payments. To complete your purchase, you will provide your credit card number directly to the payment gateways. The payment gateway's privacy policy will apply to the information you provide on the respective payment gateway's website.

  • Correcting and Updating Your Personal Information

    Our application includes links to other Websites whose privacy practices may differ from those of PayWhere's. If you submit personal information to any of those sites, your information is subject to their privacy statements. We encourage you to carefully read the privacy statement of any Website you visit. If you post a comment or respond to a posting on a social network, blog or Website, you should be aware that any personally identifiable information you submit there can be read, collected, or used by other users of those forums, and could be used to send you unsolicited messages.

  • Links to Other Websites

  • Security

    The security of your personal information is important to us.

    We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

    In order to make a purchase from our merchants, you must use the payment providers to finalize and pay for your order. Its privacy statement and security practices will also apply to your information. We encourage you to read that privacy statement before providing your information.

    If you have any questions about the security of our application, you can contact us at security@tackthis.com.

If we make any material changes to this policy that affect your personal information, we will notify you by email (sent to the e-mail address specified in your account) or by means of a notice on our application prior to the change. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
